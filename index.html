Artists

  • S.P.Balasubrahmanyam
    Sripathi Panditaradhyula Balasubrahmanyam is an Indian singer and composer. Born on 4 June 1946. He is an Indian playback singer, television presenter, music director, actor and film producer who worked predominantly in Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Hindi and Malayalam films. He is widely considered one of the greatest Indian singers of all time and he is also known as the "Gaana Gandharva". He recieved Padma Bhushan, Padma Vibhushan, Padma Shri along with many filmfare awards. He passed away in the year 2020 .

  • LataMangeshkar
    Lata Mangeshkar was born, as Hema Mangeshkar on 28 september 1929. She was an Indian playback singer and occasional music composer. She is known as "Queen of Melody", "Nightingale of India", Voice of the Millennium". She recieved Bharat Ratna, Padma Bhushan, Padma Vibhushan along with many filmfare awards. She passed away in the year 2022.

  • ArijitSingh
    Arijit Singh is an Indian singer and composer. Born on 25 April 1987. He sings predominantly in Hindi and Bengali. He is also known as the "King of playback singing". He began his career by participating in contemporary reality shows. He is the recipient of a national award & six Filmfare awards.
